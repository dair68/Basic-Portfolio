About Me

I am an aspiring programmer currently studying applied math at UCLA. Topics I enjoy include computation, differential equations, and mathematical modeling. I love solving puzzles and taking on new challenges. I've made programs in C++, Java, and Python, and aim to add more programming languages to the list!

In my free time I like to play videogames and the piano. My favorite videogames include Super Metroid , Shin Megami Tensei IV, and Nine Persons, Nine Hours, Nine Doors. My absolute favorite songs to play on the piano are "Lost in Thoughts All Alone" and "Bluebird Lamentation", which are from Fire Emblem Fates and Virtue's Last Reward respectively. One day I'd like to combine all of my interests and create a simple game of my own, with original musical compositions and fun puzzles.
